Provide storage

Give your users remoteStorage accounts

There is an npm package available for building custom remoteStorage servers, called remotestorage-server. It is the basis of the starter-kit and will be updated whenever new versions of the spec come out.

To add remoteStorage support to your online storage product, please check out our forum category and follow our latest IETF Internet Draft: draft-dejong-remotestorage-03.

All existing providers are still using one of the previous two versions, draft-dejong-remotestorage-01 and draft-dejong-remotestorage-02, but historical versions 2010.12, 2011.04, 2011.12, 2012.04, and draft-dejong-remotestorage-00 are now deprecated.
