remoteStorage

An open protocol for per-user storage

Own your data

Use a storage account with a provider you trust, or set up your own storage server. Move house whenever you want.

Stay in sync

Apps sync your data across all of your devices, from desktop to tablet to smartphone, and even your TV.

Compatibility & Choice

Use the same data across different apps. Create a to-do list in one app, and track the time on your tasks in another one.

Offline first

Use apps offline on the go, and automatically sync when you're back online. Try apps before syncing data.
